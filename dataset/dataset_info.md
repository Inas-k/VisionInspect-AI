## Defect Descriptions

### pcb_defect_1 — Scratch
Wide diagonal scratch running from top-left to board centre, crossing 6+ copper
traces. Highly visible as a continuous dark line under edge detection.

### pcb_defect_2 — Broken Track
Four horizontal and two vertical traces interrupted by burnt / oxidised gaps.
Produces strong step-edge contours at each break point.

### pcb_defect_3 — Missing Holes
Seven via drill-holes are absent (copper disk with no centre hole) and three
pads are completely missing. Detectable as anomalous circular blobs.

### pcb_defect_4 — Copper Blob
Five irregular copper blobs with solder bridges bridging adjacent signal traces.
Produces large, irregular closed contours above the defect area threshold.

### pcb_defect_5 — Multiple Defects
Combines: diagonal scratch (top-left), broken track (right side), copper blob
(bottom centre), missing hole cluster (top right), and oxidation burn mark.
Highest defect coverage — ideal for demonstrating FAIL classification.
